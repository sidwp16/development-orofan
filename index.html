ABOUT US

Really good at computers? Even if it’s just playing games and what-not? Well, THADDEUS POMPIDOU (or THAD) wants to teach you how to code. And all you have to do is play along. TEACH AND PLAY is his small way of giving back. The New York-educated code whiz wishes to divide his time between his U.S. tech-incubator job and teaching the kids of TAGPURO BOYS SHELTER in the province of Tacloban in the Philippines. At TEACH AND PLAY, it’s fun, it’s relaxed, it’s playful. It’s all about KIDS TEACHING KIDS.

CLASSES

At TEACH AND PLAY, learners basically learn BASIC PROGRAMMING in a loose, welcoming, low-pressure play-based environment.
